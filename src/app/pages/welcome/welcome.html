About This Project

"Code This, Not That" teaches Angular best practices through direct, side-by-side comparisons of anti-patterns versus recommended approaches. Each section features live, interactive demonstrations that show you the differences in action. Learn by contrast: understand not just what to do, but why the alternatives fall short.
